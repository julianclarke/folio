Fratapus

The future of fraternity rush.

Fratapus is a web-based fraternity recruitment application designed to streamline the entire rush process for brotherhoods, and particularly recruitment chairs. we had several of the ordinary rush issues ourselves and decided it was time to start doing rush better. We made Fratapus to solve them.

Beta Theta Pi

Making a more successful recruitment through promotion.

Playing off of the iconic Ray Ban brand, I was able to draw more attention to the fraternity's rush process, which saw over 300 attendees at multiple events. This exceeded the next closest house by almost 100.

SyncID

SyncID is a digital rolodex concept.

SyncID was never realized, but the User Experience and Interaction design was something I wanted to fully flesh out. The project gave me experience in crafting workflows and making a truly engaging experience.

Washington University Intefraternity Council

Branding and Identity.

As IFC President, I realized our image was entirely outdated and lacked a clear presence on campus. Through creating this identity and building a brand among peers and through fraternity engagement, I strengthened the Greek community and ultimately the student body.

Milton Glaser

Book Design

This book chapter design was the result of a research project on famous designers. Milton Glaser was an inspiration for me for his use of simplicity and ingenious messaging. He influences my work every day.

Black Label London

Marketing Concept

Black Label London is a concept for a cologne marketing campaign, featuring censored faces of England's biggest icons. It's bold, attention-grabbing, and would perhaps look iconoclastic, popular for London's emerging young professionals.

SportsBoard

Marketing + Strategy

SportsBoard is a small Bay Area startup focused on revolutionizing the amateur sports data landscape. It's the first product of its kind, so it deserves an identity equally as unique. This was an advertisement that ran in convention and showcase handouts in order to reach potential softball clients. This was one of the several ways I influenced our strategy while an intern at SportsBoard.
